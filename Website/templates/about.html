Our Goal

During the 48 hours, all of us worked togheter to create something that we would be proud of. We split our tasks. Durly and Lazăr would be creating an website to display the algorithm that Teo, Rareș and Maf would be working on. The team has learned a lot from this experience, from using OpenCV to working with Django and most importantly, coding togheter, in sync. We believe that it's not the task we've done that is amazing, but how we came togheter to accomplish such a feat.
